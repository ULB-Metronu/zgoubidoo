gamma_to_pv¶

zgoubidoo.physics.gamma_to_pv(gamma: float, particle: zgoubidoo.commands.particules.ParticuleType = <class 'zgoubidoo.commands.particules.Proton'>) → pint.quantity.build_quantity_class.<locals>.Quantity[source]¶

Converts relativistic gamma to kinetic energy.

>>> gamma_to_energy(100.0).to('MeV')
<Quantity(92888.93096999999, 'megaelectronvolt')>
Parameters:
  • gamma – relativistic gamma (dimensionless)
  • particle – the particle type (default: proton)
Returns:

kinetic energy
